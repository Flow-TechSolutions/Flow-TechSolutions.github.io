How It Works

A simple workflow designed for hotels that want less paper, fewer mistakes and better daily control.

1

Booking Email Arrives

A booking confirmation or request arrives in the hotel Gmail account.

2

AI Extracts Booking Details

The system reads dates, guest name, number of guests, room type, price and booking source.

3

Human Confirmation

Unclear bookings are marked for review. The hotel confirms important entries before they become final.

4

Google Sheets Is Updated

The booking, room status, revenue and guest overview are updated automatically.

5

Daily Monitoring

The system checks for missing data, double bookings, unpaid stays and operational issues.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Hotelmanagement-Flow.

What if the service stops?

Your data remains in your Google account. Since we only set up the automation layer, you keep full access to all your booking sheets, guest records, and historical data even if the service stops.

Do you have access to my guest data?

No. We set up the automation in your Google Workspace account, which you control. Critical data like passwords and sensitive information are reviewed by your staff before being stored.

Can my staff get training?

Yes. Our Professional and Custom packages include remote support and staff training. We help your team understand the system and troubleshoot common issues.

What if we have special requirements?

The Custom package is designed for hotel-specific setups. We can build advanced workflows, custom dashboards, and reports tailored to your hotel's operations.

Do you support multiple hotels?

Yes. We can set up Hotelmanagement-Flow for multiple properties. Contact us for bulk pricing and multi-hotel management features.

How long does setup take?

Setup typically takes 2-5 days depending on your hotel's size and complexity. We guide you through Gmail configuration, sheet setup, and AI automation rules.
